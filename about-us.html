ABOUT D.P.JEWELLERS

It was the year 1940. India was fighting to gain freedom from the Britishers. In those days of struggle and valour, Late Sh. DhoolChand Ji Kataria set out on his own struggle. He laid the foundation stone of D.P. Jewellers in Ratlam. Late Sh. Panna Lal Ji Kataria and Late. sh. Manoharlal ji kataria took this journey further which continues to unfold into new destinations & new avenues with every generation.

The brand came into being with very strong values of purity & transparency at its core and over the years, we've only built up on that ethos to earn a reputation of being the most trustworthy and honest jewellers. Under the leadership of Mr. Ratanlal Kataria, Mr. Anil Kataria and Mr. Vikas Kataria we work with a customer centric approach to offer the purest jewellery at the most competitive prices. The entire range of jewellery has a perfect balance of practical & affordable designs that are in tune with the evolving aesthetic sensibilities of India.

From being a household name in Ratlam, we're gradually expanding to capture the larger Indian market with 3 flagship stores fully operational in Indore, Bhopal & Udaipur. Catering to an ever-growing loyal customer base, we're dedicatedly working towards making D.P. Jewellers a synonym for trust, purity & honesty.

MISSION AND VISION

Ever since the inception of DP Jewellers, our single minded focus has been to provide pure jewellery at the purest and most honest prices. The future course of our journey will also be guided by the same underlying principles. We're constantly experimenting with designs to bring a versatile collection of jewellery on table that has something to offer to everyone.

Our aim is to build a brand that stands high on the pedestals of quality and transparency while being the go to destination for buying any kind of jewellery from gold to diamond, traditional to contemporary and heavy weight to light weight. Following a Customer First approach, we aspire to be the market leaders of high value, low loss jewellery that's an asset for our buyers.

Journey To Indore & Beyond!
2012-2015
